    Welcome

    International conference on Industry 4.0 and Circular Economy (ICICE 2020) is an international peer-reviewed academic conference. The ICICE 2020 aims to understand the two most important industrial paradigms-Industry 4.0 and Circular Economy-driving academia and industry in recent years. The conference would provide a forum for researchers, academicians, professionals, and students from various engineering and management fields, with cross-disciplinary interests, to interact and disseminate information on the latest developments in the area of industrial engineering and management. The conference publishes original research articles, review articles and feature articles not only in the area of industrial engineering and management but also in all the areas of Mechanical engineering such as thermal engineering, machine design, and production engineering.

    ABOUT KIIT Deemed to be University

    Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT) Deemed to be University , formerly KIIT University is an institute of higher education located in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India. The institution offers under-graduate , post-graduate,doctoral courses in Engineering, Bio-technology, Medicine, Management, Law, Computer application, Rural management, Fashion, Film Studies, Journalism, Architecture and Sculpturing. It was awarded the deemed university status in 2004 and renamed as KIIT University. In 2017 it was renamed to its original name following the University Grants Commission (UGC) request to drop the "University" from the name.

    About School of Mechanical Engineering

    School of Mechanical Engineering, established in the year 1997, produces graduates who can meet the rapidly changing needs of industry which demand new skills. Current consultancy and research & development areas of the School include residual stresses in fusion welded structure, surface finish optimization by high pressure impingement cooling and CAD modelling. Material processing technology, cleaner manufacturing technology, renewable energy, automotive engineering and quality engineering & management are the other areas of interest. Research and development efforts of the School are supported by bodies like AICTE and DST, Govt. of India.
